About Us

Developer & Designer

We are the developer of PET Bottle/Paper to Points that is used inside TUP-Cavite Campus. We made the system to help the university on dealing with paper and PET bottle waste that is not being recycled properly and at the same time giving back on both the students and faculty teachers rewards according to the accumulated rewards. We strongly believe that the making of this system will be a start to a clean environment that we all wanted and we're hoping that you will too.
